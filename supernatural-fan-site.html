Supernatural Fan Site Example

Dean Winchester

Dean Winchester is the coolest character in Supernatural. During the series he gets possessed by Michael, the strongest arch angel, and also gets the mark of Cain, evening becoming a demon at some point. He also likes to eat cheese burgers and fraternize with bar people.

Sam Winchester

Sam Winchester is the other brother of the two Winchester boys. He tends to be more of a whiner than Dean. At one point he is possessed by Lucifer, he also drinks demon blood and is able to kill Demons with his mind at one point.

Castiel

Castiel is an angel who possessed Jimmy Novak to pull Dean out of the pits of H*ll. At one point during the series he becomes the temporary God, killing the arch angel Raphael. At another point he leads an army of angels against Metatron.

Gabriel

Gabriel originally enters the show and is mistaken for a "trickster", having shenanigans with the Winchester boys. He eventually reveals himself as Gabriel and makes various appearances throughout the show.

Metatron

Metatron, formerly the voice of God is an angel with aspirations. At one point he takes over heaven after casting all the angels out. He then gets put in Heaven's jail for a while before escaping, becoming a bum, and then meeting God again.

Lucifer

Lucifer, who takes the body of Nick in this photo is known by his arch angel brothers as "Luci", others as the "light bringer", "Satan", "the Serpent", "Morning Star", etc.
